Good for beginners

This paint set is good for beginners. There’s a good variety of colors and amount of paint for the price. There’s no yearly lightfastness available, just the indicator on the tubes. When dry, there’s a bit of a cheap shininess. Both of those can be mitigated with a varnish. If you’re planning on selling your art, I recommend a higher quality paint set.

Wanted better quality

Good amount of paints for the price. Disappointed in the quality. A better set with fewer colors at the same price would provide better work. Good for practice.
